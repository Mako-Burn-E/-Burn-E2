About $BURN-E

$BURN-E is an innovative deflationary token that burns itself. Each time a small amount of $BURN-E is used, a portion is sent to the fire and removed from circulation forever—reducing supply and increasing scarcity.

How it works with PlayDeFi

  1. Burned Forever: The most significant portion is permanently removed.
  2. Treasury: A portion goes into the project treasury (community fund).
  3. Burn & Reward: 75% of the treasury is burned; 25% funds a weekly drawing for active dApp users.

The project began with the support of OG Cat NFT holders, who helped bootstrap PlayDeFi and continue earning rewards. $BURN-E Burner NFT holders participate in community decisions about burns, rewards, and growth.

PlayDeFi is the engine, Burner NFTs are the ignition, and $BURN-E is the fuel. Every event entry contributes to burning tokens, strengthening the ecosystem, and creating opportunities via ongoing rewards. The more people participate, the faster the supply decreases.

At its core, $BURN-E is about community, action, and purpose—where gaming, winning, and growth are interconnected.
