How $BURN-E Benefits from PlayDeFi Integration

When $BURN-E is a host token on the PlayDeFi dApp, it gains permanent on-chain utility through fundraiser events. Every entry contributes to burning supply, rewarding winners, and strengthening the community.

Fundraiser Event Format

Each event (Stock or Custom) requires USDC + $BURN-E for entry. Participants contribute a fixed USDC amount plus 10% of that amount in $BURN-E.

Example: A $10.00 USDC entry also requires $1.00 worth of $BURN-E.

$BURN-E Distribution

  • 75% → Burned permanently
  • 25% → Awarded to the event winner
  • 14% → Allocated to Treasuries
  • 10% → Distributed to OG Cat NFT Collection holders

USDC Distribution

  • 1% → Donated to verified charities
  • 14% → Allocated to Treasuries
  • 10% → Distributed to OG Cat NFT Collection holders
  • 75% → Awarded to the event winner

$BURN-E Treasury Proceeds

Of all $BURN-E held in Treasury: 75% is burned and 25% is redistributed to the community via weekly drawings in the PlayDeFi dApp. USDC in Treasury funds operations, development, and liquidity.

Access & Governance

$BURN-E Burner NFTs provide lifetime access to $BURN-E events (no revenue share). Burner NFT holders can vote on select Treasury actions and future reward mechanics.

The Takeaway

$BURN-E on PlayDeFi establishes a deflationary, community-driven economy: every fundraiser burns supply, rewards winners, powers dApp drawings, and strengthens the ecosystem for all $BURN-E holders.
