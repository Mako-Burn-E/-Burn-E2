(How $BURN-E Benefits from PlayDeFi Integration)

When $BURN-E becomes a host token on the PlayDeFi dApp, it gains permanent on-chain utility through the fundraiser event system. In this system, every entry contributes to burning supply, rewarding winners, and strengthening the community.

Fundraiser Event Format

Each event (either Stock or Custom) requires both USDC and $BURN-E for entry. Participants must contribute a fixed amount of USDC, along with an additional 10% of that amount in $BURN-E.

Example: For a $10.00 USDC entry, participants would also need to contribute $1.00 worth of $BURN-E.

This 10% pairing applies to all events, ensuring predictable token utility, consistent burns, and scalable integration for future host tokens.

$BURN-E Distribution

  • 75% → Burned permanently
  • 25% → Awarded to the event winner
  • 14% → Allocated to Treasuries
  • 10% → Distributed to OG Cat NFT Collection holders

USDC Distribution

  • 1% → Donated to verified charities
  • 14% → Allocated to Treasuries
  • 10% → Distributed to OG Cat NFT Collection holders
  • 75% → Awarded to the event winner

$BURN-E Treasury Proceeds

Seventy-five percent of all $BURN-E held in the Treasury is burned, while 25% is redistributed to the community through weekly drawings within the PlayDeFi dApp. The USDC held in the Treasury is used to cover project expenses, including operations, development, and liquidity.

This structure promotes continuous deflation and rewards active community members through on-chain drawings displayed in the PlayDeFi dashboard.

Access & Governance

$BURN-E Burner NFTs provide lifetime access to $BURN-E events, but they do not share in the event revenue. Regarding governance, $BURN-E Burner NFT holders have the right to vote on select Treasury actions and future reward mechanics.

The Takeaway

$BURN-E on PlayDeFi establishes a deflationary, community-driven token economy where every fundraiser burns supply, rewards winners, powers daily dApp drawings, and strengthens the ecosystem for all holders of $BURN-E tokens.
